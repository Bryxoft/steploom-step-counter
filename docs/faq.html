Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about tracking your steps with StepLoom.

Getting Started

StepLoom is a simple, privacy-friendly step counter and activity tracker. It counts your steps automatically in the background, helps you set and hit daily goals, and shows your progress with clean charts, widgets, and streaks.

StepLoom is free to use, supported by ads. A one-time premium purchase is available to remove ads and unlock extra features — it's a single payment, not a subscription.

Step Tracking & Accuracy

StepLoom uses your phone's built-in motion/step sensor to count steps automatically, even when the app is closed. There's nothing to start or stop manually.

No. Step counting relies entirely on your device's motion sensor, not GPS or location services, so StepLoom doesn't track where you are.

Goals, Streaks & Widgets

Yes. You can set any daily step goal that fits your lifestyle and change it at any time from the app's settings.

You'll get a celebration animation right in the app, plus an optional notification, so you always know when you've hit your target for the day.

Your streak grows on any day you reach your step goal. If you're behind on your goal, StepLoom sends you a reminder to help you keep your streak alive before the day ends. If you don't reach your goal, the streak resets.

Yes! StepLoom includes home screen widgets that show your daily step count and progress at a glance.

Data & Privacy

Yes. Your step history, goals, and streaks are stored locally on your device — we don't upload or store this data on our servers, and there's no account tied to it.

Yes, completely. Core step tracking, history, and goals all work without an internet connection. Only ads and optional crash/analytics reporting need connectivity.

You can wipe all of your locally stored data at any time from the app's settings, or simply uninstall the app to remove everything.
